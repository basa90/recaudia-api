sap.ui.commons, sap.ui.ux3

Collection of exceptions from the OpenUI5 browser and platform support matrix, for the sap.ui.commons and sap.ui.ux3 libraries.

The sap.ui.commons and sap.ui.ux3 libraries are deprecated as of version 1.38. For more information, see Deprecated Themes and Libraries.
